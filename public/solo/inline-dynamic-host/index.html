With 90% steel construction, we're able to deliver superior durability and create a case you can keep build to build.
A sea of space fills the interior, with a grommet-less steel cable management bar splitting the front intake from the rest of the case while keeping your cables tame and hidden.
Comes complete with the basics, USB 3.0 and HD Audio, but gives you even more with high quality materials, a smart interior layout, and tons of cable management potential.
We care about the little things. Steel plated audio jacks, captive thumb screws, and over twenty cable management points are just a few of the small touches that we've added.
Measuring in at 445mm high and 432mm long, the S340 bucks the trend of tall, thick cases, enabling you to keep your PC's footprint to a minimum.
Performance is not sacrificed for size with this small mid tower boasting full compatibility with our Kraken X61 and X31 liquid coolers.
The removal of the front 5.25" bays allows us to bring an unprecedented level of neatness to the interior of your case and showcase your build with an enormous side window. Our GRID+ (sold separately) ensures that fan control is not missed despite the removal of the front drive bays.

NZXT S340 Mid Tower Case

  • High durability with 90% steel construction
  • Unique grommet-less steel cable management bar
  • Supports Kraken X31 and X61
  • High quality steel front panel audio, captive thumbscrews, and USB 3.0
  • Over 20 cable management points and an integrated stel PSU shroud

  • Used - Like New : All of the instruction booklet or artworks are within the original case and showing no wear and tear. The disc has no visible scratch and in working condition. DLC content may have already been redeemed.
  • Used - Very Good : All of the instruction booklets or artworks are within the original case but showing minimal wear and tear. The disc has light scratches and in working condition. DLC content may have already been redeemed.
